Professional Summary

Excellence in Head & Neck Oncologic Surgery

Dr. Mukesh Kumar Sah is a highly trained Head & Neck Oncologic Surgeon with comprehensive expertise in the surgical management of malignancies affecting the oral cavity, thyroid, salivary glands, larynx, and hypopharynx.

Trained at the prestigious Tata Memorial Centre in Mumbai — India's foremost comprehensive cancer center — Dr. Sah brings world-class surgical standards and evidence-based oncologic principles to his patients in Nepal. His experience spans advanced oncologic resections, reconstructive techniques including microvascular surgery, and multidisciplinary tumor board participation.

An accomplished academic surgeon, Dr. Sah is committed to mentoring future surgeons and contributing to the advancement of head & neck oncology through research and national-level academic engagement.
